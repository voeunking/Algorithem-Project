    Terms and Conditions
    1. Membership Agreement

    By becoming a member, you agree to abide by all library rules and regulations. Membership is non-transferable and valid for one year from the date of registration.

    2. Borrowing Privileges

    Members may borrow up to 5 items at a time for a period of 3 weeks. Some items may have special borrowing restrictions.

    3. Fines and Fees

    Overdue items will accrue fines of $0.25 per day per item. Lost or damaged items will be charged to the member's account at the current replacement cost plus a processing fee.

    4. Privacy Policy

    We respect your privacy. Your personal information will only be used for library purposes and will not be shared with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

    5. Code of Conduct

    Members are expected to behave in a manner that is respectful to others and the library facilities. The library reserves the right to revoke membership for inappropriate behavior.
